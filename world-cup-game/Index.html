Scoring & Rules

Points Equation

On-pitch performance scaled by pot, plus flat stage bonuses.

Total = (Wins×3 + Draws×1 + Goals×0.5 + ShootoutWins×1.5) × Mult + Bonus
  • Regular time and extra time count for Wins, Draws, Goals.
  • KO draws award 1 Draw point to both teams.
  • Penalty shootout winner gets flat +1.5 base pts (multiplied), no win/draw points.
